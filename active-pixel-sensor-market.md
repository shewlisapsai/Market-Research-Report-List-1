<p><p>The Active Pixel Sensor (APS) market comprises various types, including the Buried Photodiode structure, which enhances light sensitivity and reduces noise by placing the photodiode beneath the surface layer. The Selection Transistor facilitates individual pixel readout, allowing for faster scanning and improved control. The MOS Amplifier amplifies the signal generated by each pixel, ensuring a higher quality image by minimizing signal degradation. Together, these components contribute to advancements in image capture technology across applications like consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
