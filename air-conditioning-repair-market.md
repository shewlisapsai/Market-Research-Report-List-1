<p><strong>The Air Conditioning Repair Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole-Home</li><li>Split Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning repair market can be categorized primarily into whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home systems are designed to cool entire residences through a centralized unit, often involving ductwork. Repairs generally focus on components like compressors, evaporators, and refrigerant lines. In contrast, split systems consist of an outdoor condenser and one or more indoor units, allowing for zoned cooling. Repair needs often arise from issues in the individual components or connections, requiring specialized knowledge for effective service.</p></p>
