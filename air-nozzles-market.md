<p><p>The air nozzles market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Tank cleaning nozzles efficiently remove residue from tanks, while pneumatic atomizing nozzles facilitate precise liquid atomization using air pressure. Hollow cone nozzles produce a cone-shaped spray pattern, ideal for agricultural and industrial applications. Full cone nozzles deliver a uniform spray, suitable for cooling and washing. Flat fan nozzles create a flat spray for even coverage, and solid stream nozzles provide focused jets for targeted applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
