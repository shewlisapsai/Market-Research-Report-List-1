<p><p>The animal identification tags market comprises two main types: tags and readers. Tags are unique identifiers, often made from materials like plastic or metal, that are attached to animals for tracking and management purposes. These tags can be RFID, barcode, or ear tags. Readers are devices used to scan and interpret the information from these tags, enhancing data collection and monitoring of livestock. Together, they facilitate efficient management of animal populations in agriculture, veterinary, and wildlife management sectors.</p></p>
