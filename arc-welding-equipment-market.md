Semi-automatic systems enhance efficiency by automating certain functions while still needing operator guidance. Fully automatic machines perform all welding operations autonomously, increasing productivity and consistency, making them ideal for high-volume production environments. Each type caters to different operational needs, from craftsmanship to mass manufacturing.</p></p>
