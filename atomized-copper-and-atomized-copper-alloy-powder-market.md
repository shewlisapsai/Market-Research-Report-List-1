<p><p>The Atomized Copper and Copper Alloy Powder market is categorized into two primary types: water atomized and air atomized powders. Water atomized powders are produced by rapidly cooling molten metal with water, resulting in finer particles and higher purity; they are primarily used in applications like powder metallurgy and additive manufacturing. Air atomized powders, created using high-pressure air, provide coarser particles with varied shapes, making them suitable for applications like thermal spray coatings and electrical components.</p></p>
