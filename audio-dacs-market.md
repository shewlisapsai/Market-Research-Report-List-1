<p><ul><li>Audio A/D Converters</li><li>Audio D/A Converters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio DAC market is divided into two main types: Audio A/D converters and Audio D/A converters. Audio A/D converters transfer analog signals, such as sound from a microphone, into digital format for processing or storage, facilitating recording and editing. Conversely, Audio D/A converters reverse this process, converting digital audio files back into analog signals for playback through speakers or headphones, ensuring high-fidelity sound reproduction. Both types play essential roles in audio production, broadcasting, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
