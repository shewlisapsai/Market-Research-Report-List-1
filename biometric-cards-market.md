<p><strong>The Biometric Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Biometric Cards</li><li>Contactless Biometric Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biometric cards market comprises two main types: contact biometric cards and contactless biometric cards. Contact biometric cards require physical contact with a reader to authenticate the user, typically using a fingerprint sensor integrated into the card. In contrast, contactless biometric cards utilize RFID or NFC technology, allowing users to authenticate without physical contact by simply waving the card near a reader. Both types enhance security, streamline transactions, and significantly reduce the risk of fraud through user identification.</p></p>
