<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Board mount humidity sensors are classified into two primary types based on their output: analog and digital. Analog output sensors deliver continuous voltage or current signals that represent humidity levels, providing a straightforward interface for integrating with other analog systems. In contrast, digital output sensors transmit humidity data as discrete signals, often utilizing protocols like I2C or SPI, facilitating easier interfacing with microcontrollers and digital systems. Each type serves varied applications, depending on the complexity and requirements of the device in use.</p></p>
