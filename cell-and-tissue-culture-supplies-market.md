<p><p>The cell and tissue culture supplies market encompasses several types of growth media essential for cell cultivation. Classical media are traditional formulations rich in nutrients. Lysogeny broth is primarily used for growing bacteria, particularly E. coli, in research settings. Chemically defined media offer precise compositions for consistent results. Specialty media cater to specific cell types or applications, while serum-free media eliminate animal serum for ethical and consistency reasons. Protein-free media support culture without animal-derived proteins, ideal for certain biopharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
