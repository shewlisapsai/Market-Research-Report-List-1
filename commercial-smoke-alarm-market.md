<p><strong>The Commercial Smoke Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarm</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarm</li><li>Dual Sensor Smoke Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial smoke alarm market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Photoelectric Smoke Alarms** utilize a light beam to detect smoke particles, providing faster alerts for smoldering fires. </p><p>2. **Ionization Smoke Alarms** rely on radioactive material to sense ion changes caused by flames, making them more effective for detecting fast-flaming fires. </p><p>3. **Dual Sensor Smoke Alarms** combine both technologies, offering comprehensive detection for various fire types, ensuring enhanced safety for commercial environments. Each type serves specific needs, balancing sensitivity and response time.</p></p>
