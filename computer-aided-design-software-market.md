<p><p>The Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software market is primarily divided into two types: On-Premise and On-Cloud solutions. On-Premise CAD software is installed locally on a user's hardware, providing direct control over the system and data security but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, On-Cloud CAD software offers flexibility and accessibility through internet-based services, allowing users to collaborate in real time from different locations while benefiting from lower initial costs and automatic updates. Each type serves different organizational needs.</p></p>
