<p><p>Constrained Layer Damping (CLD) is an advanced vibration control technique used to reduce noise and enhance the structural integrity of materials. It involves a viscoelastic layer sandwiched between two rigid layers, which dissipates vibrational energy when subjected to dynamic loads. This technology finds application across various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction, where noise reduction and material longevity are critical.</p><p>The Constrained Layer Damping Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period. Recent trends also indicate a rising interest in eco-friendly damping materials, spurred by sustainability goals within industries.
