The market is segmented into Single-Stage and Double-Stage Cycloidal Gearing. Single-Stage Cycloidal Gearing offers a straightforward design with a single reduction ratio, ideal for applications requiring moderate torque. Conversely, Double-Stage Cycloidal Gearing features two stages of reduction, providing higher torque output and efficiency, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types are crucial in robotics, automotive, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
