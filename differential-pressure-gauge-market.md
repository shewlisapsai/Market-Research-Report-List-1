<p><strong>The Differential Pressure Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston</li><li>Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The differential pressure gauge market is primarily categorized into two key types: piston and diaphragm gauges. Piston gauges utilize a movable piston to measure pressure differences, offering high accuracy and robustness, suitable for harsh conditions. In contrast, diaphragm gauges employ a flexible diaphragm that deforms under pressure, providing precise readings for a variety of applications, including gases and liquids. Both types are essential in industries like oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and HVAC for monitoring process efficiency and safety.</p></p>
