<p><p>Digital analytics software is categorized into two primary market types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. </p><p>On-Premises solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. </p><p>In contrast, Cloud-Based analytics software is hosted on remote servers, enabling easy access from anywhere and facilitating automatic updates and scalability. This model typically involves a subscription fee and lowers initial costs, making it a popular choice for many organizations seeking flexibility.</p></p>
