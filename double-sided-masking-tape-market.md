<p><p>The double-sided masking tape market can be segmented into three main types based on adhesive composition: silicon-based, acrylic-based, and rubber-based adhesives. Silicon-based adhesives are known for their high-temperature resistance and durability, making them suitable for automotive and electronics applications. Acrylic-based adhesives offer strong bonding characteristics and excellent UV resistance, ideal for outdoor use. Rubber-based adhesives provide immediate tack and strong adhesion to various surfaces, commonly utilized in general-purpose applications. Each type caters to specific needs and industries.</p></p>
