<p><p>The drilling fluid additives market includes various types of chemicals essential for optimizing drilling operations. **Fluid Viscosifiers** enhance fluid thickness to improve cuttings transport. **Corrosion Inhibitors** protect equipment by preventing metal degradation. **Dispersants** break down solid particles to maintain fluid stability. **Surface Modifiers** alter surface properties to reduce friction and wear. **Defoamers** eliminate foam to ensure smooth fluid flow. **Biocides** control microbial growth, preventing contamination and maintaining fluid integrity. Each additive plays a critical role in ensuring efficient, safe, and cost-effective drilling processes.</p></p>
