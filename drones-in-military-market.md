Radio Remote Control drones are operated directly by a human using a transmitter, offering real-time maneuverability. Automatic Program Control drones autonomously follow pre-defined flight paths, enhancing operational efficiency. Integrated Control drones combine both remote control and autonomous capabilities, allowing for versatile missions while adapting to changing environments. These types enhance surveillance, reconnaissance, and combat capabilities, significantly impacting modern military operations.</p></p>
