<p><p>The e-liquids market consists of three main types: PG-based, VG-based, and blended PG/VG e-liquids. </p><p>PG (propylene glycol) base e-liquids offer a throat hit similar to smoking, providing a stronger flavor but less vapor production. VG (vegetable glycerin) base e-liquids produce thicker clouds and a sweeter taste, making them popular among cloud chasers. Blended PG/VG e-liquids combine the best of both worlds, balancing flavor intensity and vapor production, catering to diverse preferences in the vaping community.</p></p>
