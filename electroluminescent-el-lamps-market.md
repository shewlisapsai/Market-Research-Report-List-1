<p><p>Electroluminescent (EL) lamps are a type of lighting technology that emits light in response to an electric current passing through a phosphorescent material. This technology is known for its ability to produce a soft, even glow without emitting heat, making it energy-efficient and safe for various applications. EL lamps are widely utilized in automotive displays, backlighting for screens, and in various decorative and architectural lighting solutions.</p><p>The market for EL lamps is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient lighting solutions and advancements in manufacturing technologies. The automotive sector continues to adopt EL lamps for dashboard lighting and displays, while consumer electronics benefit from their use in backlighting.
