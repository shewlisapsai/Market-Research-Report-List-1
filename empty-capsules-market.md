Non-gelatin capsules, often made from plant-based materials like hydroxypropyl methylcellulose (HPMC), cater to vegetarian and vegan consumers. Both types are widely used in the pharmaceutical and dietary supplement industries, providing effective delivery mechanisms for active ingredients and enhancing product appeal across diverse consumer bases.</p></p>
