<p><p>The excipients market is categorized into organic and inorganic excipients. Organic excipients, derived from natural sources, are often used for their compatibility, bioavailability, and functionality in formulations like pharmaceuticals and food products. They include polymers, sugars, and fats. In contrast, inorganic excipients, which are synthesized from minerals or chemicals, are predominantly utilized for their stability, inertness, and cost-effectiveness.
