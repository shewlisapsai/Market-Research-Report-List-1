<p><p>The exfoliators and scrubs market is divided into two primary types: natural and chemical. Natural exfoliators use organic ingredients like sugar, salt, or ground nuts to physically slough off dead skin cells, promoting a gentle, eco-friendly approach. In contrast, chemical exfoliators rely on active compounds such as alpha-hydroxy acids (AHAs) and beta-hydroxy acids (BHAs) to dissolve dead skin cells at a molecular level, offering deeper penetration and targeted treatment. Both types cater to varying skin needs and preferences.</p></p>
