<p><p>Farm tractor seats are available in various types, primarily categorized into mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats utilize springs and shock absorbers to reduce vibrations and enhance comfort during operation, providing a reliable and durable option for farmers. In contrast, air suspension seats use air-filled bladders to offer adjustable support and cushioning, allowing for customized comfort levels. Both seat types aim to improve operator comfort, reduce fatigue, and enhance productivity while working on the farm.</p></p>
