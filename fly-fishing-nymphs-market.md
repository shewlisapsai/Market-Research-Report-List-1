<p><p>Fly fishing nymphs are categorized into several types, each serving distinct fishing techniques. Non-beadhead nymphs are lightweight, ideal for shallow waters. Beadhead nymphs feature a weighted bead, aiding in faster sinking, perfect for deeper pools. Euro nymphs focus on high-sticking techniques, relying on thin lines and specialized patterns for a more sensitive presentation. Other nymph types may include articulated or specialty patterns designed for specific species or conditions, enhancing versatility in various fishing environments.</p></p>
