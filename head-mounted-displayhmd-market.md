<p><ul><li>Slide-on HMD</li><li>Discrete HMD</li><li>Integrated HMD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Head Mounted Displays (HMDs) are devices worn on the head to provide immersive visual experiences. The market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Slide-on HMD**: These can be attached to existing glasses and offer enhanced viewing without substantial modifications.</p><p>2. **Discrete HMD**: These are standalone devices that offer compactness and portability, often used in professional settings.</p><p>3. **Integrated HMD**: These are self-contained units that combine display, sensors, and processing power, providing a comprehensive solution for virtual and augmented reality applications.</p></p>
