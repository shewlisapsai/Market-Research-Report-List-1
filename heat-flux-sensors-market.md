<p><p>The heat flux sensors market comprises three main types: convective, radiative, and conductive heat measurement. Convective measurement sensors assess heat transfer through fluids, capturing energy exchange between surfaces and moving air or liquids. Radiative measurement sensors focus on energy transfer through thermal radiation, useful in high-temperature environments. Conductive heat measurement sensors detect heat flow through solid materials, essential for applications in building insulation and electronic devices. Each type plays a critical role in various industries, enhancing thermal management and efficiency.</p></p>
