<p><p>The hydrocarbon solvents market is primarily categorized into two types: aliphatic and aromatic. Aliphatic hydrocarbons, which include linear or branched structures, are characterized by their straight-chain configurations and are often used in applications such as degreasing and paint thinner. Aromatic hydrocarbons, on the other hand, contain conjugated double bonds, providing enhanced stability and solvent properties. They are commonly used in industrial applications like adhesives, coatings, and pharmaceuticals, where their solvency and evaporation rates are crucial for performance.</p></p>
