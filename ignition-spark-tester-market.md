<p><p>The Ignition Spark Tester market is divided into two primary types: Without Plug Spark Testers and With Plug Spark Testers. Without Plug Spark Testers are designed for testing ignition systems without connecting directly to the spark plug, providing a safe and efficient method for diagnostics. In contrast, With Plug Spark Testers are directly inserted into the spark plug socket, allowing for more precise testing of spark output and ignition strength. Both types serve essential roles in automotive maintenance and troubleshooting.</p></p>
