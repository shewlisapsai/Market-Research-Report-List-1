<p><ul><li>Aryl Aminoalcohol Compounds</li><li>Antifolate Compounds</li><li>Artemisinin Compounds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The malaria drugs market comprises several key categories: Aryl Aminoalcohol Compounds, Antifolate Compounds, and Artemisinin Compounds. Aryl Aminoalcohol Compounds, such as lumefantrine, act by disrupting the parasites' ability to metabolize hemoglobin. Antifolate Compounds, like sulfadoxine and pyrimethamine, inhibit folate synthesis, preventing parasite growth. Artemisinin Compounds, derived from the sweet wormwood plant, are highly effective and work rapidly to kill malaria parasites. Each category plays a vital role in malaria treatment strategies, addressing different resistance patterns and infection stages.</p></p>
