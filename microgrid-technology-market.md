<p><strong>The Microgrid Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied Type Microgrid</li><li>Independent Type Microgrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microgrid technology encompasses primarily two market types: Grid-Tied Microgrids and Independent Microgrids. Grid-Tied Microgrids are connected to the larger utility grid, allowing for electricity exchange and enhanced reliability during peak demand or outages. In contrast, Independent Microgrids operate autonomously, often utilizing renewable energy sources to provide localized power without dependence on the central grid. Each type serves distinct applications, addressing energy resilience, sustainability, and efficiency in various settings, including urban, rural, and industrial environments.</p></p>
