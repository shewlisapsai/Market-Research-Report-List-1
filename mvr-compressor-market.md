<p><p>The MVR (Mechanical Vapor Recompression) compressor market is primarily categorized into two types: centrifugal and Roots compressors. Centrifugal compressors utilize kinetic energy to compress vapor, offering high efficiency for large-scale applications. In contrast, Roots compressors operate using positive displacement, effectively handling viscous fluids with low energy consumption, making them suitable for smaller, specific tasks. Both types are integral in processes such as crystallization, distillation, and drying, driving energy savings and emission reductions in various industries.</p></p>
