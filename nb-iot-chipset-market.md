<p><strong>The NB-IoT Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>In-Band</li><li>Guard Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NB-IoT chipset market is divided into three primary types: Standalone, In-Band, and Guard Band. Standalone NB-IoT operates independently on dedicated spectrum, ensuring reliability and enhanced coverage. In-Band utilizes existing LTE spectrum alongside traditional cellular networks, facilitating seamless integration with current infrastructure. Guard Band allows for NB-IoT deployment within the unused frequencies between LTE channels, optimizing spectrum usage. Each type offers distinct advantages, tailored for various applications and deployment scenarios in the growing IoT landscape.</p></p>
