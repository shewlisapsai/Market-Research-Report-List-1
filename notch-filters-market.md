<p><p>Notch filters are essential electronic components used to eliminate specific frequency signals while allowing others to pass through. The market for notch filters can be categorized into tunable and non-tunable types. Tunable notch filters offer flexibility, allowing users to adjust the rejection frequency based on requirements, making them ideal for applications needing dynamic frequency control. In contrast, non-tunable notch filters are fixed at a designated frequency, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for applications with stable frequency interference.</p></p>
