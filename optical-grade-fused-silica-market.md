<p><p>The optical grade fused silica market is segmented into three primary types: UV grade, IR grade, and others. UV grade silica is designed for applications requiring high transmittance in the ultraviolet spectrum, making it ideal for photolithography and UV optics. IR grade silica, on the other hand, caters to infrared applications, providing low absorption and high thermal stability for laser and optical systems. The "others" category encompasses specialized grades that may meet unique client specifications or niche applications, further diversifying the market.</p></p>
