<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade sodium bicarbonate market is segmented into fine and coarse types based on particle size and application. Fine sodium bicarbonate, with its smaller particle size, is typically used in formulations requiring a more uniform distribution and quick dissolution, making it ideal for effervescent tablets and intravenous solutions. Coarse sodium bicarbonate, on the other hand, has larger particles and is often utilized in bulk applications, such as antacid preparations and food additives, due to its slower dissolution rate.</p></p>
