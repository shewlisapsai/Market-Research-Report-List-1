<p><p>The pulling and tensioning equipment market includes three main types: Pullers, Tensioners, and Puller-Tensioners. Pullers are designed to exert force to move or lift heavy loads, often used in construction or maintenance tasks. Tensioners, on the other hand, are utilized to apply tension to cables or wires, ensuring stability and structural integrity. Puller-Tensioners combine both functions, allowing for efficient operation in applications where both pulling and tensioning are required, enhancing productivity in various industrial settings.</p></p>
