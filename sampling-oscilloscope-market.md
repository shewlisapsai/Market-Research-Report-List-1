<p><p>The sampling oscilloscope market is primarily divided into two types: digital oscilloscopes and analog oscilloscopes. Digital oscilloscopes capture and process electrical signals using advanced digital technology, offering features like high bandwidth, memory depth, and complex analysis tools, making them ideal for modern applications. In contrast, analog oscilloscopes display waveforms in real-time as continuous signals, providing intuitive visual feedback but lacking the advanced functionalities of digital counterparts. Both types cater to different user needs within electronics testing and research contexts.</p></p>
