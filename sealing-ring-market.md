<p><p>The sealing ring market is categorized into various types based on material properties. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) is known for its excellent oil resistance. HNBR (Hydrogenated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) offers enhanced thermal and chemical stability. SIL (Silicone) provides superior flexibility and temperature resistance. VITON (Fluoroelastomer) is recognized for its exceptional resistance to harsh chemicals and high temperatures. FLS (Fluorosilicone) combines silicone and fluoropolymer advantages. Other materials include EPDM and PTFE, catering to specific industrial applications with unique sealing requirements.</p></p>
