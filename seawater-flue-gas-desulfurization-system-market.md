<p><strong>The Seawater Flue Gas Desulfurization System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet FGD System</li><li>Dry FGD System</li><li>Semi-Dry FGD System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Seawater Flue Gas Desulfurization (FGD) System market comprises three main types: Wet FGD System, Dry FGD System, and Semi-Dry FGD System. Wet FGD Systems utilize seawater to absorb sulfur dioxide from flue gases, effectively promoting high removal efficiency. Dry FGD Systems employ dry sorbents to capture sulfur emissions without water, making them suitable for plants with limited water resources. Semi-Dry FGD Systems combine elements of both wet and dry processes, using a slurry to enhance sulfur capture while minimizing water usage.</p></p>
