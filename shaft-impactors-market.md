<p><strong>The Shaft Impactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Shaft Impactors</li><li>Vertical Shaft Impactors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Shaft Impactors market is divided into two main types: Horizontal Shaft Impactors (HSIs) and Vertical Shaft Impactors (VSIs). HSIs are designed for primary and secondary crushing, using a horizontal rotor to enhance particle size reduction and shape. They are favored for their versatility and efficiency in processing various materials. VSIs, on the other hand, utilize a vertical rotor to propel material against a hard surface, producing finer products and better shaping, making them suitable for high-quality sand and gravel production.</p></p>
