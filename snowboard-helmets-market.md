<p><p>The snowboard helmet market offers three main types: full shell, half shell, and full face helmets. Full shell helmets provide comprehensive coverage, protecting the entire head, including the ears, making them ideal for aggressive riders or colder climates. Half shell helmets offer a more lightweight option, covering only the top of the head, perfect for casual riders seeking comfort and style. Full face helmets offer maximum protection with a full coverage design including a chin guard, suitable for competitive snowboarding and extreme conditions.</p></p>
