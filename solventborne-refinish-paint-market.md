<p><p>The solventborne refinish paint market comprises several key types: clearcoat, basecoat, primer, and sealer. Clearcoat provides a protective, glossy finish that enhances the underlying color. Basecoat adds color and can be specific to various hues and effects. Primer prepares the surface for painting, ensuring better adhesion and durability. Sealer, often used after basecoat application, protects against environmental damage and enhances gloss. Together, these components play crucial roles in automotive refinishing, contributing to aesthetics and longevity.</p></p>
