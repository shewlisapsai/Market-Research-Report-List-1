<p><strong>The Spare Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Tube</li><li>Tubeless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spare tire market is primarily categorized into inner tube and tubeless types. Inner tube tires consist of a separate inflatable tube secured within the tire, offering ease of repair and compatibility with various rims. On the other hand, tubeless tires are designed without an inner tube, sealing directly to the rim, which reduces the risk of air loss and enhances performance. The choice between the two depends on factors such as vehicle type, intended use, and convenience for the user.</p></p>
