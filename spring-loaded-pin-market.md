<p><ul><li>Surface Mounted Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li><li>Floating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spring-loaded pin market can be categorized into three main types: surface mounted, through hole, and floating types. Surface mounted pins are attached directly to the PCB surface, offering a compact design ideal for tight spaces. Through hole pins are inserted into drilled holes, providing strong mechanical connections and are suitable for heavy-duty applications. Floating type pins feature a design that allows for slight movement, accommodating misalignment and enhancing connectivity reliability. Each type caters to specific design requirements and performance needs in electronic assemblies.</p></p>
