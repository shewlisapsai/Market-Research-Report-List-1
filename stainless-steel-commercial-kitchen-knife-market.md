<p><ul><li>Stamped Blade Products</li><li>Forged Blade Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel commercial kitchen knife market is primarily divided into two types: stamped blade products and forged blade products. Stamped blade knives are cut from a single sheet of steel, resulting in lighter and more affordable options. They are typically easier to produce and sharpen. In contrast, forged blade knives are created through a complex heating and hammering process, offering superior strength, balance, and durability. This makes them favored by professional chefs seeking high performance and longevity in their tools.</p></p>
