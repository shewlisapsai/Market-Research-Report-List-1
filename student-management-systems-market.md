<p><p>Student Management Systems (SMS) are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easy access from any internet-enabled device, making them ideal for institutions seeking efficient management without heavy upfront costs. Conversely, on-premises systems are installed locally on school servers, providing more control over data and customization but requiring significant infrastructure investments and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to different institutional needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
