<p><ul><li>Monophase</li><li>Threephase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submergible mixer market is primarily divided into monophase and three-phase categories. Monophase mixers utilize single-phase power, making them suitable for smaller applications and environments with limited power supply. They are typically quieter and easier to install. In contrast, three-phase mixers are designed for larger operations, offering greater efficiency, power, and capacity, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, including wastewater treatment, chemical processing, and agricultural sectors.</p></p>
