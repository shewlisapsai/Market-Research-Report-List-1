<p><p>The Sun Control Window Film market is categorized into three main types. Clear (Non-Reflective) films provide UV protection without altering visibility, making them ideal for maintaining natural light. Dyed (Non-Reflective) films absorb solar heat and reduce glare while preserving aesthetics, suitable for residential applications. Vacuum Coated (Reflective) films utilize metallic coatings to reflect heat and enhance privacy, making them effective for commercial environments. Each type serves specific needs in energy efficiency, comfort, and appearance.</p></p>
