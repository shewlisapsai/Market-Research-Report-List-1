<p><p>The supraglottic device market encompasses various airway management tools essential for maintaining airway patency during anesthesia or emergencies. Laryngeal mask airways are designed to fit over the larynx, providing a secure airway without invasive intubation. Oropharyngeal airways facilitate airflow by preventing tongue obstruction in unconscious patients. Nasopharyngeal airways, inserted through the nostril, provide alternative access for ventilation. Other supraglottic devices include newer innovations and variations aimed at enhancing safety and efficacy in airway management across clinical settings.</p></p>
