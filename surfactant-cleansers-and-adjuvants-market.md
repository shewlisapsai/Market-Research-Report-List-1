<p><p>Surfactant cleansers and adjuvants are essential in formulating personal care and household products. Anionic surfactants, characterized by their negative charge, are effective at removing dirt and oils, making them popular in shampoos and laundry detergents. Cationic surfactants, with a positive charge, are primarily used for their conditioning properties, often found in conditioners and fabric softeners. Both categories play vital roles in enhancing product performance, ensuring effective cleaning, and improving user experience through their tailored properties.</p></p>
