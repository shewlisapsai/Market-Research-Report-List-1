<p><p>The tactile actuator market includes various types, primarily focusing on ERM (Eccentric Rotating Mass) actuators and LRAS (Linear Resonant Actuators). ERM actuators create vibrations through a rotating mass, commonly used in smartphones and gaming controllers for haptic feedback. LRAS, on the other hand, utilize linear movement to produce precise tactile responses, ideal for applications requiring quick, short bursts of feedback. Other types of actuators encompass piezoelectric and pneumatic options, catering to diverse applications in consumer electronics and automotive sectors.</p></p>
