<p><ul><li>Single Coated Adhesive Tape</li><li>Dual Coated Adhesive Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal release tape market includes two main types: single coated adhesive tape and dual coated adhesive tape. Single coated adhesive tape features an adhesive on one side, allowing for easy application and removal from surfaces, ideal for temporary bonding or protection. In contrast, dual coated adhesive tape has adhesive on both sides, providing a strong, permanent bond between multiple surfaces. Both types are extensively used in electronics, automotive, and construction industries for their thermal stability and effective adhesion properties.</p></p>
