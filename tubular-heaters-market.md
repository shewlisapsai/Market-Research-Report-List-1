<p><p>The tubular heaters market is segmented into single-ended and double-ended types based on their design and application. Single-ended tubular heaters have one connection point, suitable for compact spaces and straightforward heating needs. They are often used in applications where ease of installation is critical. In contrast, double-ended tubular heaters feature two connection points, allowing for enhanced heat distribution and efficiency. These are ideal for larger systems and applications requiring higher wattage or uniform heating across a broader area.</p></p>
