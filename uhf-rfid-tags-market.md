<p><p>The UHF RFID tags market includes two main types: UHF dry inlay RFID tags and UHF wet inlay RFID tags. UHF dry inlay tags are designed for integration into various surfaces without adhesive, making them suitable for applications like labeling and inventory management. In contrast, UHF wet inlay tags are manufactured with adhesive backing, allowing for direct application onto products or packaging. Both types facilitate efficient tracking and improved supply chain management across various industries, including retail, logistics, and healthcare.</p></p>
