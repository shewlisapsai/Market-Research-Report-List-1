<p><p>Vane anemometers, used for measuring wind speed and airflow, are categorized into two main types: integral and split. Integral type vane anemometers feature a single, compact design, combining the measuring components and display, making them portable and easy to use for field applications. In contrast, split type vane anemometers consist of separate components, where the sensor can be positioned at the measurement site while the display unit is located away, allowing for greater flexibility in various environments.</p></p>
