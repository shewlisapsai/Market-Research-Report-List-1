<p><p>The vibratory separator market consists of three primary types based on layer configuration: single-layer, double-layer, and triple-layer types. Single-layer vibratory separators are designed for simple applications, efficiently separating materials in one pass. Double-layer units enhance separation effectiveness by providing two screening surfaces, accommodating varying particle sizes simultaneously. Triple-layer separators maximize efficiency further, offering three distinct levels for complex separation tasks. These types cater to diverse industries, improving productivity and precision in material classification and processing.</p></p>
