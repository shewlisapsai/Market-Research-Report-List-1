<p><p>The viral vector development service market encompasses various types of vectors used in gene therapy and research. Retroviral vectors are utilized for stable gene integration but have limitations in cell type targeting. Lentiviral vectors, derived from HIV, enable stable expression in dividing and non-dividing cells. Adenoviral vectors offer high transduction efficiency but do not integrate into the host genome. Adeno-associated viral vectors are known for their safety and long-term expression capabilities. Other viral vectors include those tailored for specific therapeutic applications and research needs.</p></p>
