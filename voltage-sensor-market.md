<p><strong>The Voltage Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Output by Pin</li><li>Output by Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage sensor market can be categorized into two main types based on output configuration: output by pin and output by wire. Output by pin sensors provide voltage readings directly through dedicated pins on the sensor, facilitating easy integration into circuit boards and systems. Conversely, output by wire sensors transmit voltage data through insulated wires, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and distance from the monitored source. Both types cater to different application needs, enhancing monitoring and controlling capabilities in various sectors.</p></p>
