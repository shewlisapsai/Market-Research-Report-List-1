<p><strong>The Wafer Dicing Protective Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The wafer dicing protective film market is segmented into two main types: Non-UV Film and UV Film. Non-UV Film is designed for general protection during the dicing process without exposure to ultraviolet light, providing a reliable barrier against contaminants. In contrast, UV Film utilizes ultraviolet light for curing, offering enhanced adhesion and durability while allowing for easier handling during the dicing process. Both types play crucial roles in safeguarding wafers and ensuring precision in semiconductor manufacturing applications.</p></p>
