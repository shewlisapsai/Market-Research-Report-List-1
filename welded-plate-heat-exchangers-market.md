<p><strong>The Welded Plate Heat Exchangers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-welded</li><li>Fully-welded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Welded plate heat exchangers are categorized into two main market types: semi-welded and fully-welded. Semi-welded heat exchangers consist of a combination of welded and gasketed plates, allowing for easier maintenance and cleaning, making them suitable for applications with varying fluids. Fully-welded heat exchangers feature all plates welded together, ensuring higher durability and leak resistance, ideal for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Both types offer efficient thermal performance, catering to various industrial needs, including chemical processing and power generation.</p></p>
