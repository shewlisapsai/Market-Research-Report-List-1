<p><p>The wiper blade market is categorized into three main types: boneless, bone, and hybrid wiper blades. Boneless wiper blades feature a sleek design without a metal frame, providing improved aerodynamics and a quieter operation. Bone wiper blades consist of a metal frame, offering durability and effective performance in extreme weather. Hybrid wiper blades combine elements of both designs, featuring a metal frame covered with a rubber shell, ensuring enhanced visibility and all-weather performance. Each type caters to specific consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
